I am a Ph.D. student at the Information, Risk and Operation Management Department at McCombs School of Business at the University of Texas at Austin. My current research explores fairness and interpretability in Machine Learning (ML) Systems. As part of my work, I characterize how bias may creep into data labels for training a supervised learning system, and how such label bias may lead to unwanted consequences without awareness. Moreover, I explore methods that mitigate label bias and promote bias aware active data collection. Generally speaking, in my research, I aim to understand the limits and risks of using ML systems to assist decision-making, and to develop methods to bring accountability to ML assisted decisions making from both algorithm design perspective and ML designers' behavior perspective.
